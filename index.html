Mathematical Background

This simulator visualizes the evolution of probability densities under the flow of a one-neuron ReLU neural ODE:

ẋ = max(0, wx + b)

The closed-form solution for the density evolution is:

ρ(x,t) = ρ₀((x - bt)e^(-wt)) · e^(-wt) for wx + b > 0
ρ(x,t) = ρ₀(x) · δ(x + b/w) for wx + b ≤ 0
